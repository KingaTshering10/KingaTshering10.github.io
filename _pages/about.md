<div class="about-card-title">Research Direction</div>

My work spans **vision-language model evaluation**, **sparse subnetworks for
hyperspectral image classification**, **few-shot open-set recognition in medical
imaging**, **LLM-based computational reasoning**, and **explainable machine
learning** for pediatric screen-time overuse prediction.
